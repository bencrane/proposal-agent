# Direct Mail — Supplementary Capability

## What It Is
Physical mail sent to prospects — typically used for high-value accounts or as part of a multi-channel ABM strategy.

## When to Include
- High-value B2B targets where email alone may not break through
- When the prospect has a physical product that could benefit from a tangible touchpoint
- Account-based strategies targeting a small number of high-value accounts

## Positioning
- Premium add-on, not a default inclusion
- Positions the engagement as a multi-channel GTM strategy
- Usually reserved for larger engagements or higher-price-point deals
