# Cold Calling — Supporting Capability

## What It Is
Outbound phone calls to prospects, typically used to supplement cold email campaigns and drive higher conversion rates.

## How It's Used
Cold calling is NOT sold as a standalone service. It is a cost the firm absorbs to drive better outcomes (and thus more revenue via performance fees). However, mentioning that "we also cold call" in a proposal justifies higher pricing.

## Positioning in Proposals
- Include as a capability that enhances the engagement
- Frame as: "Our team also conducts targeted outbound calls to warm leads and high-value prospects"
- Never position as a separate line item
- Use it to justify higher per-outcome fees or setup fees

## When to Include
- When the prospect's market has prospects who are more responsive to calls (e.g., restaurant owners, bar managers)
- When the primary outcome requires a higher-touch approach
- When justifying premium pricing
