# Inbound IVR — Capability

## What It Is
Two variants:
1. **Live Transfer** — Inbound calls routed live to the client's sales team
2. **Scheduled Callback** — Prospects request a callback at a specific time

## When to Include
- When the client's sales process benefits from live phone conversations
- Staffing, insurance, financial services, home services — industries where phone is king
- When the primary outcome IS a phone conversation (live transfer or scheduled call)

## Positioning
- This is the outcome in some verticals (e.g., "pay per live transfer")
- For other verticals, it's a conversion mechanism downstream of email/calling
- Premium capability — higher cost per outcome reflects higher value per lead
