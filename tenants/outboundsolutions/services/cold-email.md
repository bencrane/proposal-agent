# Cold Email — Primary Service Capability

## What It Is
Dedicated outbound email infrastructure engineered to reach a client's ideal target market at scale. This is the primary capability and the one most proposals center on.

## What We Build
- Dedicated sending infrastructure (domains, inboxes, warmup)
- Proprietary data pipelines for lead sourcing and validation
- Campaign systems designed to test offers and iterate at speed
- Every variable is measurable and tunable: copy, audience segment, send timing, domain health

## Scale
- Up to 50,000 emails per month (including follow-ups) per engagement
- Multiple campaign types can run in parallel — different offers, segments, asks

## Key Positioning
- We are an engineering firm, not a marketing agency
- We build infrastructure, not templates
- The firms that win at outbound are the ones that can launch, test, and adapt faster than anyone else
- We generate more booked meetings at lower cost per meeting than hiring in-house or alternative agencies

## How It's Sold
Cold email is the foundation of most engagements. It is NOT sold as "cold email services" — it's sold as an outcome delivery system. The client buys results (meetings, sample requests, etc.), and cold email is the engine underneath.
