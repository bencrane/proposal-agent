# Your Role — Standard Section

We handle everything upstream — infrastructure, data, list building, validation, copy, sending, deliverability, reply triage. Your team only sees warm leads.

What you need to do:
- **Take the meetings.** When we hand off a warm lead, respond fast. Speed matters.
- **Fulfill promptly.** If the engagement involves samples or demos, have inventory and a process ready.
- **Provide feedback.** Tell us what's converting and what's not. The faster the feedback loop, the faster we optimize.
- **Be available for kickoff.** The Build phase requires your input on targeting, messaging, and offer approval.

The better your team executes on warm leads, the better the results. We bring them to the door — you close it.
