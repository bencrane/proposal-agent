# The Engagement — Standard Phases

## Build (Weeks 1–3)
Everything that has to exist before a single outreach email is sent. Infrastructure, data, lists, copy, creative — all built from scratch during a 3-week onboarding period.

### Infrastructure Setup
Dedicated sending domains, inboxes, and warmup sequences. Fully isolated from our other clients.

### Data Sourcing & List Building
Proprietary data pipeline to source, validate, and segment your target market.

### Campaign Strategy & Copywriting
Offer development, sequence design, A/B test framework, and approved copy.

### Creative Assets
Landing pages, response capture forms, and any supporting collateral.

## Launch (Ongoing)
Campaigns are live and generating conversations. This is the ongoing operational layer — volume, scheduling, rotation, reply triage, and lead handoff.

### Campaign Execution
Emails sent, sequences running, domains rotated, volume managed.

### Reply Management & Lead Handoff
Positive replies triaged, qualified, and handed off to your team with context.

## Optimize (Continuous)
Continuous iteration on infrastructure health, copy performance, and targeting precision. Every week of live data makes the next week more effective.

### Infrastructure Health
Domain reputation monitoring, inbox rotation, deliverability optimization.

### Copy Performance
A/B test analysis, subject line iteration, offer refinement based on response data.
