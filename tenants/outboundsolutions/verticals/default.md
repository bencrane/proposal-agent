# Default Vertical Context

When no specific vertical applies, use this general context.

## Primary Outcome
Booked meetings with decision-makers at the prospect's target companies.

## Typical Buyer
- B2B company with an identifiable ICP
- Currently doing outbound manually or not at all
- Founders or sales leaders who are spending time on prospecting instead of closing

## Common Pain Points
- Founders doing their own outreach (opportunity cost)
- Hired SDRs who can't send at volume
- Tried agencies that didn't deliver
- Paid ads too expensive for B2B meetings

## Pricing Tendency
- Setup + performance is the natural fit
- $3,000–$5,000 setup, $150–$300 per booked meeting
